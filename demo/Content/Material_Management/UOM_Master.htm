    UOM Master

    This refers to the unit of measurement in which the items are stored, sold and purchased.

    UOM is being captured in 'Item master' and 'Drug Product Master' screens. Could set the UOM for Purchase (used at the time of purchasing), Sale (used while issuing), and stock UOM( used while updating stock).

    Screen:

    MM Masters -> UOM Master (masters login)

    Table:

    uom_master

    Fields:

    UomCode, TextBox

    This refers to the code in which the specific Uom is identified .This serves as the primary key.

    Description, TextBox

    This refers the description the user can enter for identification.

    BaseUnit, DropDown

    This refers to the base unit in which the items are measured.

    Active, Checkbox

    To activate or inactivate a uom.

    Conversion, TextBox

    Conversion field specifies how many base units does this uom contain. It is used in application to convert units, when both uoms are different and base_unit_id same.

    Unit conversion needed while doing transactions like GRN, PurchaseOrder.

    Quantity in Purchase Order is specified in Purchase UOM.

    On receiving stock for the item from vendor, quantity is converted to Stock uom, since stock is maintained in Stock UOM.

    E.g.: Let Purchase UOM - Box. (means item purchased as BOX), Stock UOM - Nos (means item stored as Nos). In this case, before updating thestock, quantity in BOX is converted to Nos.

    The conversion column is used only when the purchase and sale uoms are different and the base_uom_id is the same, other wise it is not used

    Normally, stock and sale UOM are same.

    Default conversion value set as 1.

    Entry should be made in to Item Unit Conversion, if base UOMs are different.

    Example

    uom_master:

    idDescriptionBase_Unit_IdConversion

    219 Pairs12

    Horlicks is purchased as no of jars and stocked and sold as no of jars. Here the conversion is not used.

     

    Eg.2

    Item_master

     

    Iddescriptionstock_uom_idsale_uom_idpurchase_uom_id
    14 Steel 6 4 * 45cm CV-320 50mm6969133

    Here the item is purchased in a box of 12 items and sold as a foil of 1 item each.

    Hence at PO creation time 1 means 1 box.

    At GRN time the stock will be updated in foils and entered as 12 foils by multiplying with the conversion factor. Here the conversion factor is used.

    Note: The UOM’s are very user requirement specific.

    Action buttons and operations specific for this page is

    Create, for creating a new Entry

    Add the corresponding items in the fields listed above and click on the create button

    This will help us to create a new entry for the uom

    While creating a new entry UomCode ,Description and BaseUnit are

    the fields to be entered mandatory

    Search

    Searching can be possible by entering any one of the details

    Enter the details of the item u want to search and click on the search button the corresponding items/item will be displayed on the screen.

    Clear

    Clearing the contents of the field

    Close

    Closing the page
