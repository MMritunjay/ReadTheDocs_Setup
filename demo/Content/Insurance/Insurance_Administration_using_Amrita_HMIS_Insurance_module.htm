    Insurance Administration using Amrita HMIS Insurance module

    Before going through the Amrita HMIS Insurance module in detail, one should understand that the Amrita Insurance Module is only a part of Amrita HMIS’s Insurance Administration, Billing and Claims Management and not a stand alone modulesubscription management module. It is part of the Amrita HMIS’s larger Insurance Administration, Authorization Management, Patient Billing and Claims Management. When Insurance administration is done using Amrita HMIS Insurance Module, the authorization management, Patient Billing and Claims management are done using the Amrita Patient Billing Solutions Module. Now let us go through the key features as well as the key terms used in the module in brief.

    Key Features

    The module is tailor made to manage the various insurance requirements of a hospital which manages different customized insurance schemes, for its patients as well as its employees, in tie up with various insurance companies. Combined with Amrita HMIS billing and claims management module it becomes a powerful module in managing the day to day insurance and claim activities in a hospital. This module facilitates:

    1. Storing Insurance Company Details (Primary Contact Details)

    2. Storing Details of companies who subscribe to the policies offered by the hospital

    3. Storing different types of policies and shemes

    4. Capturing different policy rules

    5. Capturing promoter details

    6. Managing subscriptions including Premium collection, renewal, expiry, transfer etc:-

    7. Capturing demograpic and insurance details of your:

    • Employees

    • Students

    • Insured general public and

    • Dependants, where ever required; as part of the subscription

    8. Printing premium receipts

    9. Validating premium payment against policy activation

    10. Printing out insurance ID cards once the policy is activated and all details are received.

    Key Terms used in Amrita HMIS Insurance

    • Company Master

    • Policy Option

    • Insurance Category Master

    • Insurance Sub-Category

    These terms are explained in detail in the coming sections of the document.
