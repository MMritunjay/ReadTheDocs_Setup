    Blood Ordering

    Blood Ordering is integrated with ‘HIS’.

    The blood components needed by the patient can be ordered through this.

    • The blood ordering screen can be taken from 'My Patient Browser' (My Patients menu - nurse/mrdv login) or directly from Create Blood Orders menu.
    • Blood Ordering screen can be taken from IP\My Patient Browser for nurses as well as directly from menu.
    • Once created, new components cannot be added to the existing order. For this, new order has to be created.
    • At least one 'Blood unit' in the order should be issued to close Blood Order.
    • On selecting speciality, speciality unit combo can be made visible when app.config - ADT-AUTO_POPULATE_UNIT_DOCTOR is true.
    • Ordering in ml/unit:
      • By default ordering unit will be 'ml' , and for privileged user can change it to 'units'.
      • Privilege needed is BB-CHANGE_BLOODBAG_AND_BATCH.
      • If order is created in 'units' , blood issue also in units. i.e a blood component considered as a single unit and full volume issued as 1 unit.
      • App. config - BB-DEFAULT_BLOOD_ORDER_UNIT is used to defaultordering unit.
