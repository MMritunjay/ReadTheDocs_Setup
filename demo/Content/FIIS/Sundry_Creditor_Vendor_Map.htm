    Sundry Creditor Vendor Map

    The material management related activities happen in the Amrita MM sub-system. Hence, the vendor master is maintained in Amrita MM sub-system. A ledger of the vendor is created under the Accounts Payable (Sundry Creditors) in Amrita FIIS. The invoices are first captured in the Amrita MM Sub-system and posted to the Amrita FIIS. It is later on settled from the Amrita FIIS. For smooth integration workflows between these systems, mapping of Vendors created in MM to Ledgers created in FIIS is necessary. Please follow the steps below to do the same.

    1. Select ‘Vendor Account Mapping’ sub-menu from the ‘Masters’ main-menu.

    2. A page ‘Vendor - Account Mapping’ appears. Combo boxes ‘Vendor’, ‘Accounts Payable’ along with buttons ‘Search’, ‘Add new Mapping’, ‘Add New Vendor’ and ‘Clear’ are visible on the page.

    3. To map a vendor created in the materials Management module to a Sundry Creditor created in the FIIS module, use ‘Add new Mapping’ button. Select the vendor name and ledger name from the respective combos on the left and right side of the pop up page.

    4. Click Map to complete the process

    5. Click ‘Clear’ to clear the screen.

    New Vendor using Vendor Account Mapping

    Users can add new vendors from the ‘Vendor - Account Mapping’ page. It will result in adding a new vendor to the vendor list of ‘Materials Management’ module and creation of a new ledger account under the group ‘Sundry Creditors’. The mapping of vendor with ledger is automatic here. Steps to be followed are:-

    1. Use ‘Add New Vendor’ button

    2. A new page ‘Vendor / Manufacturer Master’ comes as a pop up

    3. Fill the relevant fields (Note:- Some are mandatory fields) and use ‘Save’

    4. Use ‘Close’ to Close the pop up page
