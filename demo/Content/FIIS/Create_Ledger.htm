    Creating a Ledger

    To create a ledger:

    1. From the ‘Finance Masters, main menu, select ‘Create Accounts’.

    The Ledger Master screen appears with all the account groups in the left panel. On the right panel, the page is displayed with search, Add, Edit, Delete and Clear buttons.

    2. Select an Account Group/Sub-Account Group on the Left Han Panel. You can see accounts created under the group on the Right Hand Side.

    3. Click on Add.

    New Ledger screen appears.

    4. The Account Group/Sub-Account Group already selected can be seen appearing by default against the field Under.

    5. Enter the name of the ledger.

    6. Click Next to proceed.

    Opening Details of the ledger is activated provided the ledger to be created belongs to Account Group Type ‘A’ or ‘L’.

    7. Click Add to add the opening details.

    The Update and Cancel buttons becomes active and Add, Edit and Delete button becomes inactive.

    8. Enter the following details:

    Reference

    Date

    Business Area

    Profit Centre

    Cost Centre

    Amount

    Dr/Cr

    Remarks

    9. Click Save to save the opening details.

    The opening details get listed in a separate panel at the bottom of the window.

    10. Click Other to enter the following address and tax details:

    Address

    Phone

    Fax

    Contact Person

    Email ID

    ST No

    ST Date

    CST No

    CST Date

    11. Click Save.

    Displays the confirmation message

    Note:-

     Ledgers can only be created against the leaf node or lowest level sub-group

     ‘Opening Details’ button will only be active if the ledger to be created belongs to Account Group Type ‘A’ or ‘L’.

     

    Other Tasks

    To search a ledger

    1. Select ‘Create Accounts’ sub-menu from the ‘Transaction’ main menu

    Ledger master page appears where account group on the Left Hand Side and a search button along side a user entered field on the Right Hand Side can be seen.

    2. Enter the first few letters of the ledger you want to find

    3. Click ‘Search’

    4. The ledger name with opening balance if any will appear on the data table.

    To edit a ledger:

    1. Select the ledger from the Ledger Master screen.

    2. Click Edit.

    New Ledger screen appears.

    3. Make the necessary changes and click Save.

    Displays the confirmation message.

    To delete a ledger:

    1. Select the ledger from the Ledger Master screen.

    2. Click on Delete.

    3. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    Displays the confirmation message.

    Note:A ledger which doesn’t have any transactions can only be deleted.
