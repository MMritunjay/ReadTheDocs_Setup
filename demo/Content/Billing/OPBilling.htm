    Creating a Bill

    Most scenarios discussed here apply for patients in “general” category that pay for most services. Since BPL patients do not pay for any service, the following sections are not applicable to their order processing.

    You can generate a bill for an inpatient or an outpatient using the Amrita HMIS application’s billing services. The HMIS application keeps track of all the services rendered to the patient, such as lab tests, and materials issued; determines the price per service; processes any advance payments made by the patient; generates a bill; and produces a receipt upon payment of the bill amount. Outpatients are provided with a bill at the time of their visit and inpatients at the time of their discharge.

    The OP patients are required to pay for the services and materials on the day of visit itself. All the charges for lab test, materials, and other services of the IP patients are posted to the final bill which is to be paid at the time of discharge.

    As the medicines are issued free of cost, pharmacy billing is not required for IP and OP patients but no patients are exempted from material billing.

    The Amrita HMIS system provides multiple billing windows to help you process inpatient, outpatient, and material bill. Each of these operations can be performed by selecting the corresponding commands from the Billing menu. Following is an example of a billing window, followed by a brief description of each field and option available in the window.

    The terminology “final bill” frequently used in the application to refer to a consolidated bill that includes all components serviced to the inpatient.

    1. Creating an OP Bill

    Outpatients (OP) require bill payment prior to rendering any service. Since OP patients are not admitted to the hospital, they pay for the services provided to them at the time of their visit. Before any services ordered for an outpatient is processed, it has to be entered into the system first. A bill is generated and the patient goes to the payment counter to pay the bill before the service is rendered. The HMIS OP billing services helps you ensure that bills are processed and payments are made in a timely fashion.

    1.1 Creating an OP Bill

    You can create and save the OP bill of a patient. The OP bill saved can be searched using the bill browser. Services can be added to the bill which is already saved. Also the required services can be removed from the bill at the time of bill creation.

    OP Bill Window

    1. Select OP Billing from the Billing main menu.

    OP Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Uncheck the check box Bill Prepared.

    5. Click Save to save the bill.

    A confirmation message appears to create and save a new bill.

    6. Click OK of the confirmation message.

    7. The bill is saved and appropriate confirmation message appears on the screen.

    The Print Bill and Cancel Bill button appears in addition to the existing buttons.

    NOTE: The status of the bill changes to Open. At this point of time, changes can be made to these bills.

    1.2 Confirming an OP Bill

    Confirming a bill refers to finalizing it prior delivering it to the patient. Once the final bill is confirmed, neither new services nor new material items can be added or removed from the bill.

    1. Select OP Billing from the Billing main menu.

    OP Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Check the check box, Bill Prepared.

    5. Click Save.

    Displays the confirmation message for creating and confirming the bill.

    6. Click Ok of the message.

    Displays another confirmation message.

    7. Click Ok of the second confirmation message.

    The bill is confirmed and appropriate message appears on the screen. The buttons Save, Remove, and Add New Service disappears from the screen. The buttons Print Bill, Cancel Bill Preparation and Approve Discharge appears on the screen.

    1.3 Removing Items From the OP Bill

    You can remove the items from the bill before saving the bill.

    1. Select OP Billing from the Billing main menu.

    OP Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Select the bill item which is to be removed.

    5. Click Remove.

    A confirmation message appears on the screen.

    6. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The selected item is removed from the bill.

    1.4 Adding New Services During OP Bill Creation

    In case a patient was given services or materials that did not get billed, these can be added to the bill at the payment counter prior to pay. These can be termed miscellaneous and thus add them to the bill.

    1. Select OP Billing from the Billing main menu.

    OP Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Click Add New Service.

    Add Miscellaneous Service screen appears.

    5. Enter the following details.

     Service Name

     Date

     Quantity

     Rate

    6. Check the checkbox Add as a Package if the entered service is to be added as a package.

    7. Click Ok.

    The new service details added appears in the IP Final Bill screen.

    1.5 Accepting Cash Against OP Bill

    Once the OP bill for the patient is created and confirmed, you can accept the cash against the bill.

    1. Create and Confirm the OP bill for the patient using the OP Billing option from the Billing main menu.

    2. Click Accept Cash.

    Displays a confirmation message.

    3. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The payment is accepted against the bill and a confirmation message appears on the screen. The Accept Cash button is replaced by the Save button.

    NOTE: The status of the bill changes to Closed.

    1.6 Viewing the Payments Made by OP Patients

    The prior payments made by the OP patient can be searched at the time of creating the OP bill and IP final bill for the patient.

    1. To view the prior payments made by the OP patient, click Show Payments in the OP Bill screen. To view the prior payments made by the IP patients, click Show Payments in the IP Final Bill screen.

    Receipts screen appears.

    2. Enter any of the following search criteria:

     MRD Number

     Receipt Number for the bill paid. This information is not mandatory

     From Date

     To Date

    3. Check For Current Visit Only to search for the receipts of current visit.

    4. Click Search.

    The payment receipts meeting the search criteria appear on the screen.

    5. Click Print against each payment receipt to print the receipts.

    The bill payment receipt opens up in pdf format.

    1.7 Browsing for OP Bills

    The billing clerk may need to retrieve existing bills for a multitude of reasons. Bill browser allows you to find bills that may be currently open, confirmed, pending or closed.

    1. Select Bill Browser from the Billing main menu.

    Bill Browser screen appears.

    2. Enter any of the following search criteria:

     MRD Number

     Bill Type (OP Bill)

     Bill Number (optional)

     Visit Code (optional)

     From Date

     To Date

     Bill Status (optional)

     Bill Payment Status (optional)

    3. Click Search.

    The bills meeting the search criteria appear on the screen.

    4. Click on the link for any bill no. to open the bill.

    Any active bill can be accessed using the bill browser.

    1.8 Cancelling the Saved OP Bill

    You can cancel an OP bill which is created and saved.

    1. Search for a saved OP Bill using the Bill Browser.

    2. Select the bill to be cancelled. The details are displayed in OP Bill screen.

    3. Click Cancel Bill.

    The bill gets cancelled.

    1.9 Cancelling the Confirmed OP Bill

    You can cancel an OP bill which is created and confirmed.

    1. Search for a confirmed OP Bill using the Bill Browser.

    2. Select the bill to be cancelled. The details are displayed in OP Bill screen.

    3. Click Cancel Bill Preparation.

    A confirmation message appears on the screen.

    4. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The bill gets cancelled.

    1.10 Printing the OP Bill

    Once the OP bill is created and saved, you can take the printout of the bill.

    1. Create and Save the OP bill for the patient using the OP Billing option from the Billing main menu.

    2. Click Print Bill.

    The bill opens in pdf format.
