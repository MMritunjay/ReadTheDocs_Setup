    Creating an Inpatient Final Bill

    The charges of the services rendered to the inpatient get posted to the final bill. The patients admitted in the nursing homes pay an advance amount at the time of admission. Prior to creating a bill, advances paid by the patient can be searched using the bill browser. The advance amount, and any amount refunded to the patient is also shown up in the IP final bill.

    The IP Final Bill Window enables you to create bill, confirm bill, accept cash, and print the bill. You can prepare and save the IP final bill for the patient. Once the IP bill is saved, you can add services to it, if required. Once a bill is confirmed as final, it is frozen.

     

     

    1.1 Creating Inpatient Final Bill

    You can create and save the final bill. The final bill saved can be searched using the bill browser. Services can be added to the bill to any existing saved bill. Also the required services can be removed from the bill at the time of bill creation.

    1. Select Create Final Bill from the Billing main menu.

    IP Final Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Uncheck the check box Bill Prepared.

    5. Click Save to save the bill.

    A confirmation message appears to create and save a new bill.

    6. Click OK of the confirmation message.

    7. The bill is saved and appropriate confirmation message appears on the screen.

    8. The Print Bill and Cancel Bill button appears in addition to the existing buttons.

    NOTE: The status of the bill remains Open.

    1.2 Confirming Inpatient Final Bill

    Confirming a bill refers to finalizing it prior delivering it to the patient. Once the final bill is confirmed, neither new services nor new material items can be added or removed from the bill.

    1. Select Create Final Bill from the Billing main menu.

    IP Final Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Check the check box, Bill Prepared.

    5. Click Save.

    Displays the confirmation message for creating and confirming the bill.

    6. Click Ok of the message.

    Displays another confirmation message.

    7. Click Ok of the second confirmation message.

    8. The bill is confirmed and appropriate message appears on the screen. The buttons Save, Remove, and Add New Service disappears from the screen. The buttons Print Bill, Cancel Bill Preparation and Approve Discharge appears on the screen.

    NOTE: The status of the bill changes to Confirmed.

    1.3 Removing Items From an IP Final Bill

    You can remove the items from the bill before saving the bill.

    1. Select Create Final Bill from the Billing main menu.

    IP Final Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Select the bill item which is to be removed.

    5. Click Remove.

    A confirmation message appears on the screen.

    6. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The selected item is removed from the bill.

    1.4 Adding New Services during IP Final Bill Creation

    In case an inpatient was given services or materials that did not get billed during his/her admission, these can be added to the bill at the payment counter prior to pay. These can be termed miscellaneous and thus add them to the bill.

    1. Select Create Final Bill from the Billing main menu.

    IP Final Bill screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    3. Click Details.

    The name, visit code, category, and bill details appear on the screen.

    4. Click Add New Service.

    Add Miscellaneous Service screen appears.

    5. Enter the following details.

     Service Name

     Date

     Quantity

     Rate

    6. Check the checkbox Add as a Package if the entered service is to be added as a package.

    7. Click Ok.

    The new service details added appears in the IP Final Bill screen.

    1.5 Viewing the Advance Paid by the Patient

    The advances paid by an IP patient can be searched at the time of creating the OP bill and IP final bill for the patient.

    1. To view the advances paid by the IP patients, click Show Advances in the IP Final Bill screen.

    Receipts screen appears.

    2. Enter any of the following search criteria:

     MRD Number

     Receipt Number (not mandatory) for the bill paid.

     From Date

     To Date

    3. Check For Current Visit Only to search for the current visit receipts.

    4. Click Search.

    The advance payment receipts meeting the search criteria appear on the screen.

    5. Click Print against each advance payment receipt to print the advance receipts.

    The advance payment receipt opens up in pdf format.

    1.6 Accepting Cash For Inpatient Services

    Once the final bill for an IP patient is created and confirmed you can accept the cash against the bill.

    1. Create and Confirm the IP final bill for the patient using the Final Bill option from the Billing main menu.

    2. Click Accept Cash.

    Displays a confirmation message.

    3. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The payment is accepted against the bill and a confirmation message appears on the screen. The Accept Cash button is replaced by the Save button.

    NOTE: The status of the bill changes to Closed.

    1.7 Viewing the Payments Made by IP Patient

    The prior payments made an IP patient can be searched at the time of creating the IP final bill for the patient.

    1. To view the prior payments made by the IP patients, click Show Payments in the IP Final Bill screen.

    Receipts screen appears.

    2. Enter any of the following search criteria:

     MRD Number

     Receipt Number

     From Date

     To Date

    3. Check For Current Visit Only to search for the receipts of current visit.

    4. Click Search.

    The payment receipts meeting the search criteria appear on the screen.

    5. Click Print against each payment receipt to print the receipts.

    The bill payment receipt opens up in pdf format.

    1.8 Browsing for IP Bills

    The billing clerk may need to retrieve existing bills for a multitude of reasons. Bill browser allows you to find bills that may be currently open, confirmed, pending or closed.

    5. Select Bill Browser from the Billing main menu.

    Bill Browser screen appears.

    6. Enter any of the following search criteria:

     MRD Number

     Bill Type (IP Bill)

     From Date

     To Date

     Bill Status (optional)

     Bill Payment Status (optional)

     Bill Number (optional)

     Visit Code (optional)

    7. Click Search.

    The bills meeting the search criteria appear on the screen.

    8. Click on the link for any bill no. to open the bill.

    Any active bill can be accessed using the bill browser.

    1.9 Printing the IP Final Bill

    Once the bill is created and saved, you can take the printout of the bill.

    1. Create and Save the IP final bill for the patient using the Final Bill option from the Billing main menu.

    2. Click Print Bill.

    The bill gets opened in pdf format.

    1.10 Cancelling the Saved IP Final Bill

    Prior to payment, you can cancel an unconfirmed IP final bill which is created and saved.

    1. Search for a saved IP Bill using the Bill Browser.

    2. Select the bill to be cancelled. The details are displayed in IP Final Bill screen.

    3. Click Cancel Bill.

    The bill gets cancelled.

    1.11 Cancelling the Confirmed IP Final Bill

    Prior to payment, you can cancel the IP final bill that is confirmed.

    1. Search for a saved IP Bill using the Bill Browser.

    2. Select the bill to be cancelled. The details are displayed in IP Final Bill screen.

    3. Click Cancel Bill Preparation.

    A confirmation message appears on the screen.

    4. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The bill gets cancelled.

    NOTE: The status of the bill changes to Cancelled.
