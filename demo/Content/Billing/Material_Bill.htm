    Creating a Material Bill

    Amrita HMIS Material Billing services helps you generate the bill for the materials consumed by OP and IP patients. Use the Material Billing window to search for the patient using the patient’s MRD number and generate a material bill. You can search for the material orders of the patient and can bill for the items in the material order.

     

     

    To create a material bill

    1. Select Material from the Billing main menu.

    Material Billing screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number.

    The name of the patient, age/sex, visit, doctor name, category, and department is automatically displayed by the AHMIS application.

    3. Click View Material Orders.

    Material Order Browser appears.

    4. Enter any of the following search criteria:

     From Date

     To Date

    5. Select Show Active Only to search for the active orders.

    6. Select the desired status for the material order such as Pending Only, Closed, Partially Processed, All Material Orders.

    7. Click Search Material Orders.

    The material orders meeting the search criteria appears on the screen.

    8. Select the desired material order and click Select.

    The items in the selected material order appear in the Material Billing screen.

    9. Click the Batch No icon to search for the batch number of the item.

    Item Selector screen opens with the available batches of the item.

    10. Select the required batch and click Select.

    The batch number, price, expiry date, stock, UOM (unit of measure) of the item appears on the Material Billing screen.

    11. Enter the quantity issued.

    The total amount, debit and/or credit are automatically displayed by the AHMIS application.

    12. Click Confirm & Issue.

    A confirmation message appears on the screen.

    13. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The bill is saved and confirmed and appropriate message appears on the screen.

    1. Creating Material Bill for Non Ordered Item

    You can bill for the items which are not in the material order.

    1. Select Material from the Billing main menu.

    Material Billing screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number.

    The name of the patient, age/sex, visit, doctor name, category, and department is automatically displayed by the AHMIS application.

    3. Click Select Material.

    Material Browser screen appears.

    4. Select the option for Material Name or Material Code.

    5. Enter the material name or material code based on the option selected.

    6. Click Search.

    The material meeting the search criteria appears on the screen.

    7. Select the material and click Add Item.

    8. The items in the selected material order appear in the Material Billing screen.

    9. Click the Batch No icon to search for the batch number of the item.

    Item Selector screen opens with the available batches of the item.

    10. Select the required batch and click Select.

    The batch number, price, expiry date, stock, UOM of the item appears on the Material Billing screen.

    11. Enter the quantity issued.

    The total amount, debit and credit is automatically displayed by the AHMIS application.

    12. Click Confirm & Issue.

    A confirmation message appears on the screen.

    13. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    The bill is saved and confirmed and appropriate message appears on the screen.

    2. Printing the Material Bill

    After creating the material bill you can print it.

    1. Create the material bill using the option, Material from the Billing main menu.

    2. In the Material Billing window, click Print Bill.

    The bill opens up in pdf format.

    3. Removing the Items from the Material Bill

    You can remove the material items, which are not to be billed from the Material Billing Window.

    1. Select the items which are to be removed from the Material Billing window.

    2. Click Remove.

    A confirmation message appears on the screen.

    3. Click Ok of the confirmation message.
