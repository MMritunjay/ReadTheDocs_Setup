    Processing Payments

    The billing module of AHMIS application enables you to process various payments such as collecting an advance from the patient say for his/her in the nursing home, refunding amount to the patient. It also helps you to view the prior payment details of the patient.

    1 Collecting Payment Advance

    Patients that admitted to the nursing home may need to pay an advance before admission. You to collect this advance from the patients using the payment screens.

    1. Select Payments from the Billing main menu.

    Payments screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    The name of the patient, visit ID, current account balance appears on the screen.

    The various bill details of the patient appears in the lower panel of the screen.

    3. Click Collect Advance.

    Multiple Payment: Accept Advance screen appears with the patient name, MRD and visit code.

    4. Select the mode of payment from the drop down, Payment Mode.

    The payment mode can be cash, cheque, credit card, or DD.

    5. Enter the required details based on the selected payment mode.

    If cheque or DD is select you should enter the Amount, Cheque/DD/CC No, Bank Name, and Expiry Date.

    If credit card is selected you should enter the Amount, Cheque/DD/CC No, Bank Name, Credit Card Authorization Code, Credit Card Holder Name and Expiry Date.

    If Cash is selected only amount need to enter.

    6. Click Add.

    7. The payment details such as receipt number, payment mode, amount remarks appear on the lower panel of the screen. The total amount appears at the bottom of the screen.

    8. Click Save.

    A confirmation message appears.

    9. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    Advance is colleted and appropriate message appears on the screen.

    10. Click Ok of the message.

    11. Click Close to close the screen.

    2 Refunding Patient Payments

    Refunding of payments are done if: 1) If the patient pays for a service and post payment, the service is not rendered, or 2) if the patient has paid an amount that is over and above the cost of the service.

    1. Select Payments from the Billing main menu.

    Payments screen appears.

    2. Enter or browse the MRD number of the patient.

    The name of the patient, visit ID, current account balance appears on the screen.

    The various bill details of the patient appear in the lower panel of the screen.

    3. Click Refund against the bill which is to be refund.

    Multiple Payment: Refund Bill screen appears with the patient name, MRD and visit code.

    4. Select the mode of payment from the drop down, Payment Mode.

    The payment mode can be cash, cheque, credit card, or DD.

    5. Enter the required details based on the selected payment mode.

    If cheque or DD is selected you should enter the Amount, Cheque/DD/CC No, Bank Name, and Expiry Date.

    If credit card is selected you should enter the Amount, Cheque/DD/CC No, Bank Name, Credit Card Authorization Code, Credit Card Holder Name and Expiry Date.

    If Cash is selected only amount needs to be entered.

    6. Click Add.

    7. The payment details such as receipt number, payment mode, amount remarks appear on the lower panel of the screen. The total amount appears at the bottom of the screen.

    8. Click Save.

    A confirmation message appears.

    9. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    Amount is refunded and appropriate message appears on the screen.

    10. Click Ok of the message.

    11. Click Close to close the screen.

    4. Select the mode of payment from the drop down, Payment Mode.

    The payment mode can be cash, cheque, credit card, or DD.

    5. Enter the required details based on the selected payment mode.

    If cheque or DD is selected you should enter the Amount, Cheque/DD/CC No, Bank Name, and Expiry Date.

    If credit card is selected you should enter the Amount, Cheque/DD/CC No, Bank Name, Credit Card Authorization Code, Credit Card Holder Name and Expiry Date.

    If Cash is selected only the amount needs to be entered.

    6. Click Add.

    7. The payment details such as receipt number, payment mode, amount remarks appear on the lower panel of the screen. The total amount appears at the bottom of the screen.

    8. Click Save.

    A confirmation message appears.

    9. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    Bill payment is accepted and appropriate message appears on the screen.

    10. Click Ok of the message.

    11. Click Close to close the screen.
