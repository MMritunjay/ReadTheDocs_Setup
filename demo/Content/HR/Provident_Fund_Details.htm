    Entering Provident Fund Details

    1. From the HR main menu, select Employee.

    2. From the Employee submenu, select PF Details.

    The PF & Nominees screen appears.

    3. Enter the employee number

    The employee name, employee type, department, work area and designation is automatically displayed by the AHMIS application.

    4. Click the tab PF to enter the following PF Details.

     PF number

     PF account number

     Total period

     Date of joining for PF fund

     VPF set on date

     VPF percentage

     Reason for resignation

     Joining remarks

     Internal Remarks

     Resignation Remarks

    5. Click Save to save the PF Details entered.

    The details you entered are saved and a confirmation message appears at the top of the screen

    The Save button becomes inactive, while the Update button becomes active.

    NOTE:Employee number is mandatory for entering PF Details.

    1 Updating PF Details of the Employee

    1. Make the required modifications for the PF details.

    2. Click Update to save the modifications.

    Displays a confirmation message at the top of the screen.

    Other Tasks

    To update the PF details of the employee:

    1. Click the Nominees tab in the PF & Nominees screen.

    2. Enter the following details:

     Nomination Type (Pension, PF)

     Name

     Percentage Due

     Relationship

     Date Of Birth

     Address

     Guardian Name

     Guardian Relationship

     Guardian Address

     Comments, if any

    3. Click Save to save the nominee details

    A confirmation message is displayed at the top of the screen and the nominee details entered is displayed in a panel below the tabs.

    The edit button in the panel below the tabs enables you to edit the nominee details.

    To enter the nominee details of the employee:

    1. Click Edit in the panel below the tabs.

    The nominee details entered is displayed in the respective boxes. Also the save button becomes inactive and Update and Delete button becomes active.

    2. Make the required changes in the nominee details.

    3. Click Update.

    A confirmation message is displayed at the top of the screen and the nominee details you entered are displayed in a panel below the tabs.

    Use the Edit button in the panel to edit the nominee details.

    To delete the nominee details of the employee:

    1. Click Edit in the panel below the tabs.

    The nominee details entered is displayed in the respective boxes. Also the save button becomes inactive and Update and Delete button becomes active.

    2. Click Delete.

    A confirmation message is displayed at the top of the screen. Now the Update and Delete button becomes inactive and Save button becomes active.
