    Maintaining Employee Information

    With the Amrita HMIS solution, all employee details are captured, stored, and managed through the HR module. Employee details that include their prior work experience, educational background, contacts are captured through the registration process. Every new hire is given a unique employee number upon registration. The employee number or name can be used to retrieve records of the benefits earned by the employee. Requests for loan or vacation can be processed and approved online. Thus from the day of employment till retirement or resignation, an employee’s personal information can be captured and tracked within the HR module.

    Employee Master

    All employee details are captured on the Service Book window. Once these details are captured and saved they can be modified at any point of time. The details are grouped into different tabs, such as Official and Personal.

    1 Registering an Employee

    1. Select Service Book from the Employee sub menu, from the HR main menu.

    The Service Book screen appears.

    2. Click the main tab to enter the following mandatory details of the employee.

     Title

     First Name

     Last Name

     Employee Type

     Organization/Unit

     Department

     HOD (head of the department)

     Reporting Officer

     Work Area

     Designation

     Discipline

     Employee Status (Confirmed, Not confirmed, Resigned)

     Employee Class (Medical Non medical)

     Date of join

     Next appraisal date

     Employee Group ( confidential, non confidential)

    3. Click Save to save the details entered.

    The employee details you entered are saved and the employee is registered and a confirmation message appears at the top of the screen,Save button becomes inactive, and the Update button becomes active.

    NOTE:Title, First Name, Last Name, Employee Type, Organization/Unit, Department, HOD, Reporting Officer, Work Area, Designation, Discipline, Employee Status, Employee Class, Date of Join, Next Appraisal Date, and Employee Group must be specified to register an employee.

    2 Modifying Employee Details

    1. Select Service Book from the Employee sub menu, from the HR main menu.

    The Service Book screen appears. Click on the main tab.

    2. Make the required modifications.

    3. Click Update button.

    Displays a confirmation message at the top of the screen.

    NOTE:The next appraisal date cannot be modified.

    3 Entering Employee Official Details

    1. Click the Official tab in the Service Book screen.

    2. Enter the following details:

     MRD number

     Attendance Card Number

     Attendance Type (Physician or Non physician)

     Next Increment Date

     Renewal Date

     E-mail

     Mobile number

     Comments

    1. Click the Address tab in the Service Book screen.

    2. Enter the following details:

     Address line 2

     Address line 3

    6 Editing Address Details of the Employee

    1. Click the Address tab in the Service Book screen.

    2. Click Edit in the panel below the tabs.

    The Add New button becomes inactive and the Delete and Update buttons become active.

    3. Make the required modifications.

    4. Click Update to save the changes.

    The HMIS application displays a confirmation message at the top of the screen. The Update and Delete button becomes inactive and Add New becomes active.

    To delete the address details of an employee:

    1. Click Edit in the panel below the tabs.

    Now Add New becomes inactive and delete and update becomes active.

    2. Click Delete to delete the address details.

    3. Click Ok of the confirmation message.

    Displays a confirmation message at the top of the screen.
