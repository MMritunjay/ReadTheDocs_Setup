    Submitting Resignation

    Use the Resignation window to submit the resignation of an employee.

    fieldDescription
    Request NumberA resignation number is automatically generated when a request for resignation is made online.
    Request Date Displays the date on which the resignation is requested.
    Employee #Enter the employee number.
    NameDisplays the name of the employee.
    DepartmentDisplays the department of the employee.
    DesignationDisplays employee’s job position in the hospital.
    Date Of JoinDisplays the joining date of the employee
    Requested Resignation DateEnter the date on which the resignation is requested.
    Resignation StatusDisplays the status of the resignation.
    Cancellation DateEnter the date on which the resignation is cancelled.
    Reason For ResignationEnter the reason for canceling the resignation.
    Expected Relieving DateEnter the date on which the employee is to be relieved of his/her job duty.
    Bond PeriodEnter the bond period of the employee
    Bond BrokenCheck whether the employee has ever broken the bond.
    Bond AmountEnter the bond amount which is to be paid by the employee.
    Receipt Date by HRDEnter the date on which the resignation is received by HR department.
    Last Payroll MonthEnter the last payroll month of the employee.
    HOD CommentsEnter the Head of the Department comments.
    HR CommentsEnter the HR comments.
    Employee CommentsEnter the comments of employee.
    Cancellation ReasonEnter the reason for canceling the resignation.
    Exit Interview OnEnter the scheduled date for exit interview.
    Clearance Amount Enter the clearance amount to be paid by the employee.

    To submit a resignation:

    1. From the HR main menu, select Employee

    2. From the Employee submenu, select Resignation Browser.

    The Resignation Details screen appears.

    3. Select Resignation from the sub menu, Employee of HR main menu.

    The Resignation window appears.

    4. Enter the Employee number.

    The name, date of join and designation is automatically populated by the AHMIS application.

    5. Enter the following details:

     Reason for resignation

     Requested resignation date

     Last payroll month

    6. Click Add New

    A confirmation message is displayed at the top of the screen and the Add New button is replaced with Update button.

    NOTE:The resignation request number is automatically generated and the resignation status is displayed as pending. The Employee Number, Requested Resignation Date, Reason For Resignation, and Last Payroll Month are mandatory for submitting a resignation.
